 # Project Overview: Nature Bloom Foods Website Development

## Project Title and Purpose

**Project Title:** Nature Bloom Foods – Responsive Business Website Development

**Purpose:**
The Nature Bloom Foods website project aims to establish a professional online presence for Nature Bloom Foods, a company focused on providing natural, healthy, and organic food products. The website addresses the business need for a centralized digital platform where customers can explore products, learn about the company, and connect with the business.

With increasing consumer demand for online product discovery and brand transparency, having a modern, responsive website is essential for improving visibility, customer engagement, and brand credibility.

---

## Key Objectives

1. Develop a fully responsive website that functions seamlessly across mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.
2. Showcase at least 8 natural food products with detailed descriptions and pricing information.
3. Improve customer engagement by providing clear navigation and accessible contact information.
4. Achieve a page load time of under 3 seconds on standard internet connections.
5. Deliver a professional and visually appealing website aligned with the Nature Bloom Foods brand identity.

---

## Project Scope

### In Scope

* Responsive homepage with hero section and company introduction.
* Product catalog displaying natural food products.
* About Us section describing company mission and values.
* Contact page with inquiry form structure.
* Navigation menu and footer across all pages.
* Mobile, tablet, and desktop responsive layouts.
* Modern UI design using HTML5 and CSS3.

### Out of Scope

* Online payment gateway integration.
* User registration and authentication systems.
* Inventory management functionality.
* Order processing and shipping management.
* Backend database development.
* Mobile application development.

## Risks and Assumptions

### Risks

1. Delays in receiving product information or images.
2. Changes in branding requirements during development.
3. Browser compatibility issues on older devices.
4. Hosting or deployment configuration challenges.
5. Future feature requests causing scope expansion.

### Assumptions

1. Product content and images will be available before development completion.
2. Stakeholders will provide timely feedback.
3. Website content will remain stable during development.
4. Hosting environment supports standard HTML and CSS deployment.
5. No backend functionality is required in the initial release.
